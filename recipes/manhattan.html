Manhattan


Photo by Emily Andreeva on Unsplash

The Manhattan is a classic cocktail dating back to the 1800's. One of the five cocktails named for a New York City borough. This time-tested cocktail consists of only three simple ingredients, rye whisky, vermouth, and bitters.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz or 60 ml of Rye Wisky
  • 1 oz 60 ml of Sweet vermouth
  • 2 Dashes of Bitters

Directions

  1. Grab a mixing glass and add some ice.
  2. Measure and pour 2 oz of rye whisky into the mixing glass
  3. Add 1 oz of sweet vermouth
  4. Next add 2 dashes of bitters
  5. Stir the drink
  6. Strain the drink into a cocktail glass
  7. optional garish: maraschino cherry on a cocktail pick
