Mint Julep


Photo by Ari Agustian on Unsplash

The mint julep cocktail has been the official drink of the Kentucky Derby since the 1930's, but the mint Julep is actually much older dating back to the early 1800's. This is a classic recipe consisting of only three ingredients, bourbon, simple syrup, and mint. You don't need to be at Churchill Downs to enjoy the drink. The mint julep is a great summer drink to enjoy throughout the season. Cheers.

Ingredients

  • 3 oz or 90 ml of bourbon
  • Mint leaves
  • 1/2 oz or 15 ml of simple syrup

Directions

  1. Muddle a pinch of mint leaves (8-10 leaves) and simple syrup in a glass
  2. Add bourbon
  3. Add crushed ice
  4. Gently stir
  5. Garnish with mint
