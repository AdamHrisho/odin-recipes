Bourbon Old Fashioned


Photo by Pylyp Sukhenko on Unsplash

One of the most iconic and well-known cocktails in history. The old fashioned is a traditional cocktail consisting of only a few ingredients muddled together topped off with your whisky of choice. In this version we'll use bourbon to give it a subtle sweeter profile. Cheers.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz or 60 ml of Bourbon
  • 1/2 teaspoon or 2.85 grams of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon or 5 ml of water
  • 2 Dashes of bitters
  • Garnish: Orange Peel

Directions

  1. Add the sugar and bitters to a rocks glass, then add the water. Stir until the sugar is almost dissolved
  2. Fill the glass with ice cubes (larger the ice, the better)
  3. Add the Bourbon and stir the drink
  4. To garnish, express the oil of the orange peel into the cocktail and drop it into the drink
